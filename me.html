About Me

From zero to passionate. How I develop my passion in living.

Music. When I was a little kid. I hated piano so much that my mom needed to sit with me at the piano and forced me to play it. It was a terrible time that I couldn't enjoy music at all. But when I grew up, I started to learn that music connects with my soul. I could express my feelings through the flowing melody. That was when I was in grade 7, I decided to continue my learning of music by taking violin lessons. And in the recent years, I also tapped into playing guitar and writing folk songs with it. The passion of music didn't grow in a day. It was plated in me by my parents and it grew over time. These days, I couldn't imagine a life without it.

Food. I was once those kids who never cook at home and never cared about eating. I could be eating the same dish every meal and feel nothing. Not until I moved to Toronto. I got in touch with the culinary world. I invested time to improve my cooking skills and try to perfect every dish I created to impress people. Now, cooking had developed to become my passion and something that I enjoy.
